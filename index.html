    Alberto Rizzo is an Italian sonic researcher constantly involved in the physicality of sound, bodily gestures into the ether and visual listening. His stratified practice led him to experiment with chaotic sonic spaces in which air flows and inaudible streams are shaped and painted into reality. His latest research investigates the use of AI in his feedback instrument, interrogating the controversial human controlled by machine dilemma and ways of using machine learning to create new sonic landscapes.
